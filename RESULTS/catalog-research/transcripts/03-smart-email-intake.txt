This module monitors a designated Gmail label for new Upwork job alert emails. As alerts come in, we extract the job title, ID, and relevant metadata. The system picks up the job IDs, submits them for analysis, and reviews the scoring logic. In this run, both jobs scored below our configured threshold, so they were automatically ignored.
